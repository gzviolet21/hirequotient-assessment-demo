# Case-design research notes

Retrieved 2026-06-21.

- BCG Careers, [Case Interview Preparation](https://careers.bcg.com/global/en/case-interview-preparation): BCG describes its cases as realistic business challenges that test structuring, thoughtful questions, data analysis, quick calculation, identifying the important factors, communication, business intuition, and creativity. It states that there is not always one right answer.
- The same BCG page publicly offers a **Climate Strategy** quiz. Its stated prompt is to build the business case for a company setting a climate target and determine the initiatives required to achieve it.

Design consequence for this simulator: the SuryaGrid case is fictional and uses invented figures, but it tests a researched case form: target-setting, initiative selection, data interpretation, quantitative reasoning, reliability, affordability, governance, and a conditional recommendation—not a purely financial answer.
